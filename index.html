Build the community your alumni never stopped wanting

Pecan is an AI community tool built on top of your tech stack to automate hyper-personalized alumni engagement. It links with your current databases, provides event-alumni recommendations, and automatically reaches out

Streamline primary data gathering

Pecan directly connects to your CRM and databases. It also establishes direct communication channels for informal stakeholders - student societies, professors, and adjacent teams.

Create events tailored to what your alumni want

Instead of working backwards from events to alumni, Pecan provides actionable recommendations based on your current alumni segments, learning on the go.

Automate hyper-personalized communication campaigns

Mimic high-touch comms without the need for a massive team. Pecan drafts out one email per interested alum, and autonomously reaches out.
