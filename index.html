Why Pecan

A mass email treats a thousand people as one.

Pecan writes a different email for every recipient, then rehearses each one against a behavioural model of that person before you ever hit send.

The usual way

One template, blasted to everyone.

Dear valued contact, we are pleased to invite you…
Dear valued contact, we are pleased to invite you…
Dear valued contact, we are pleased to invite you…
Dear valued contact, we are pleased to invite you…

You learn what worked only after the send, from an open rate. By then the campaign is already spent.

With Pecan

One email, written and tested per person.

Amara, the deals panel maps onto your current desk…
Jack, a robotics evening, ten minutes from your lab…
Priya, a CPD session on the regulation you flagged…
Daniel, two from your cohort have already replied…

Each email is rehearsed against a behavioural model of the recipient. You send the version that already worked.
